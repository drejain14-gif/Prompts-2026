# SweatJoy — Business Requirements Document (BRD)

## 1. Executive Summary

SweatJoy addresses the ₹50,000+ crore Indian test-prep market's critical gap: student mental health. By embedding wellness tracking into the exam preparation journey, SweatJoy creates a differentiated B2C and B2B2C product for coaching institutes, schools, and parents.

## 2. Business Objectives

1. **Reduce student burnout** in partner coaching institutes by 30%
2. **Increase student retention** for partner institutes by 15%
3. **Build a freemium SaaS model** with premium AI coaching and analytics
4. **Establish data-driven wellness insights** for institutional partnerships

## 3. Stakeholders

| Stakeholder | Interest | Influence |
|-------------|----------|-----------|
| Students | Personal wellness tools | High |
| Parents | Child safety visibility | Medium |
| Coaching institutes | Retention, reputation | High |
| Counsellors | Early intervention data | Medium |
| Investors | Scalable SaaS metrics | High |

## 4. Market Opportunity

- 40M+ students in competitive exam pipeline annually
- 70% report exam-related anxiety (NCERT survey data)
- No dominant wellness-first exam prep companion app in India

## 5. Revenue Model

| Tier | Price | Features |
|------|-------|----------|
| Free | ₹0 | Mood tracking, journal, basic habits |
| Pro | ₹199/mo | AI coach unlimited, advanced analytics, exam mode |
| Institute | Custom | Admin dashboard, bulk licensing, counsellor portal |

## 6. Key Business Rules

- Parent access requires explicit student opt-in
- Critical burnout alerts trigger counsellor notification workflow
- Student data never sold to third parties
- Minimum age: 13 (with parental consent under 18)

## 7. Compliance Requirements

- IT Act 2000 data protection guidelines
- NEP 2020 mental health alignment
- WCAG accessibility for government school partnerships

## 8. ROI Projections (Year 1)

- 50K free users → 5K Pro conversions (10%) → ₹99.5L ARR
- 10 institute partnerships → ₹50L ARR
- **Total projected ARR:** ₹1.5 Cr
